We provide a meal to the hungry. We are a not-for-profit charity helping underprivileged, homeless, vulnerable groups, social outcasts and street dwellers with a meal which they cannot afford. Shelter for a family

We hope that with the money raised we can finally build a home, fit to provide the necessary security and comfort that every human deserves to have access to. The target set, would be enough to provide the essential raw materials and labour for building a house made of concrete and large enough to accommodate the Fayez family comfortably.
